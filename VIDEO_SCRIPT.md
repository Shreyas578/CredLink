# CredLink - Video Presentation Script
## Duration: 3-4 minutes

---

## OPENING: THE PROBLEM (0:00 - 0:30)

"1.7 billion people worldwide have perfect payment histories but zero credit scores. In India, 450 million people use UPI every month—they pay bills on time, they're financially responsible—but banks won't lend to them.

And if you move countries? Your credit history vanishes. You start from scratch.

This is the problem CredLink solves."

---

## THE SOLUTION (0:30 - 1:00)

"CredLink is the world's first on-chain credit passport built on Creditcoin blockchain.

We turn your real-world payments into verifiable credit scores. Every UPI payment, every utility bill, every phone recharge—they all build your credit reputation on-chain.

Your score is portable across borders, verified by AI, and owned by you forever. No banks required."

---

## HOW IT WORKS (1:00 - 2:00)

"Here's how simple it is:

You upload a payment proof—a UPI screenshot, electricity bill, anything. Our system extracts the details using OCR, then runs AI fraud detection with Ollama's Gemma 2B model locally. We verify the merchant against our registry of 30+ trusted institutions like HDFC Bank, Jio, Amazon.

Once verified, your payment goes to IPFS for storage, and you sign a transaction to record it on Creditcoin blockchain. Your credit score updates instantly—300 to 850 range, just like traditional credit.

Your loan eligibility? Simple: your score times 20. A 550 score means you're eligible for ₹11,000."

---

## UNIQUE FEATURES (2:00 - 2:45)

"What makes CredLink revolutionary?

**Credit Delegation**: You can lend part of your reputation to help someone else. A parent helping their child, a business owner vouching for an employee. If they repay, you both win. If they default, you both lose points. Trust, tokenized.

**Merchant NFTs**: Every verified merchant has an on-chain NFT. You can't fake a payment to HDFC Bank—we verify it on the blockchain.

**Portable Identity**: Your Credit Passport is an NFT. Move to Dubai, London, anywhere—your credit history moves with you.

**Privacy-First**: Sensitive data stays on IPFS. Only your score and verification hashes live on-chain. Your data, your control."
